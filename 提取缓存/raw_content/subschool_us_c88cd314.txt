SubSchool - course creation platform
====================================

  Create courses + tutoring with AI that generates & grades homework    [Teacher Sign-Up](https://teacher.subschool.us/register)     [Student Sign-Up](https://class.subschool.us/en/register)                 

What is SubSchool?
------------------

SubSchool is an all-in-one platform for online teaching where you can create and sell courses and run tutoring without building a website or wiring payment systems.
  

* ![]()   
  ### **AI-Powered Creation & Grading**
  
  Turn lesson materials into homework, checks, and even course visuals. Generate tasks, grade essays and video answers, and save hours of routine work.
* ![]()   
  ### **Personalized Homework**
  
  Give each student the right level of challenge. Adaptive assignments adjust based on performance so learners don’t get bored—or stuck.
* ![]()   
  ### **Sell by Course, Module, or Single Lesson**
  
  Let students try one lesson before committing. Lower the entry barrier, build trust faster, and increase conversions.
* ![]()   
  ### **Entry-to-Exit Exam Rating**
  
  We measure what matters: the gap between entry and exit exams. Bigger improvement = higher course rating and stronger trust
* ![]()   
  ### **Built-In 1:1 Tutoring**
  
  Set availability, approve bookings, and run lessons from one place. A session link is created automatically—no scheduling chaos.
* ![]()   
  ### **Recordings + Homework in One Thread**
  
  Every tutoring call is saved, and homework lives right next to it—assign, submit, review, and track progress without switching tools.
      

How SubSchool works for the educator:
-------------------------------------

Easily create courses and homework assignments. Run live lessons and tutoring sessions, view statistics.
    

* ### Create your first course by simply uploading lectures
  
  AI will automatically create lesson descriptions, divide them into modules, and add homework assignments.
   [Try it](https://teacher.subschool.us/register)
* ### Transfer your routine to AI
  
  Create covers, subtitles for lessons, and homework assignments when adding lessons.
   [Try it](https://teacher.subschool.us/register)
* ### Use mathematical and chemical editors
  
  Add formulas and chemical compound diagrams when manually writing articles for lessons and describing homework assignments
   [Try it](https://teacher.subschool.us/register)
* ### AI will automatically check solutions in essay, presentation, or interview format.
  
  But you can still review the student's solution, view the AI's assessment, and correct it if necessary.
   [Try it](https://teacher.subschool.us/register)
* ### Start teaching as a tutor
  
  Create a profile and students from courses can come to you as a tutor for additional lessons.
   [Try it](https://teacher.subschool.us/register)
* ### Conduct live lessons
  
  Both one-on-one and for groups of students within courses
   [Try it](https://teacher.subschool.us/register)
        

How SubSchool work for learners:
--------------------------------

Purchase courses starting from a single lesson. Complete engaging assignments and select tutors.
    

* ### Buy courses from one lesson
  
  This way, you can see for yourself the quality of the course and whether it is right for you. You can choose only the lessons that you need right now.
   [Try it](https://class.subschool.us/en/register)
* ### Find suitable tutors and book specific times
  
  The cost will be calculated automatically, and the amount will be deducted from your balance based on the duration of the lesson recorded for the actual time spent
   [Try it](https://class.subschool.us/en/register)
* ### Join lessons and complete tasks in the chat
  
  Connect to lessons from the chat, receive lesson recordings and homework assignments for the lessons you have taken.
   [Try it](https://class.subschool.us/en/register)
* ### Read the lessons as text, watch videos and presentations
  
  Take online courses at your own pace and in your preferred format
   [Try it](https://class.subschool.us/en/register)
