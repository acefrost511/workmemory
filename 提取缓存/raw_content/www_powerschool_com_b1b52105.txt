# Advancing Personalized Learning with AI-Powered Tools 2025
### Revolutionizing K-12 Education with Generative AI and Smarter Tools
February 22, 2024
\*\*Topics:\*\* [Assessment](https://www.powerschool.com/resources/?filter\_resource\_types=post&filter\_topics=assessment), [Communications & Family Engagement](https://www.powerschool.com/resources/?filter\_resource\_types=post&filter\_topics=communications-family-engagement), [Curriculum & Instruction](https://www.powerschool.com/resources/?filter\_resource\_types=post&filter\_topics=curriculum-instruction), [Interoperability](https://www.powerschool.com/resources/?filter\_resource\_types=post&filter\_topics=interoperability), [Personalized Learning](https://www.powerschool.com/resources/?filter\_resource\_types=post&filter\_topics=personalized-learning)
A teacher’s job in the classroom is already challenging and time-consuming, and juggling many disconnected tools can make it more difficult.
Built to simplify teaching and learning, our [personalized learning solutions](https://www.powerschool.com/personalized-learning-cloud/) bring together K-12-focused platforms and AI tools to create customized learning experiences for every student. This powerful set of connected solutions combines a comprehensive assessment, curriculum, learning management, and instructional content tools. 
![](https://www.powerschool.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/1119638\_Whats-New-for-2023-Update\_Body-Graphic-1.webp)
PowerSchool’s personalized learning solutions allow educators quick visibility into knowledge gaps, individualized learning experiences for every student, and centralized access to collaborative, standards-aligned curriculum maps.  
And we’re continually improving them with the most robust, experienced edtech development team. 
We’re excited to highlight the following improvements and updates for personalized learning solutions in 2025, reflecting our focus on customer input, innovations, and evolving education needs. 
## Generative AI Enhances Assessment Creation in Performance Matters
Creating personalized assessments that accurately capture each student’s mastery can take time, but it doesn‘t have to. With PowerSchool[Performance Matters](https://www.powerschool.com/classroom/performance-matters/), teachers get a powerful ally in generative AI, making it easier to design personalized and effective assessments. Whether crafting questions, passages, or other content, everything is tailored to the right grade level, standard, and topic in one seamless workflow.
Generative AI in Performance Matters helps educators:
### Save Time
Teachers can quickly create high-quality questions, passages, and more with just a few clicks. That means less time writing and more time teaching.
### Expand Resources for Non-Core Subjects
Non-core subjects often don‘t get the resources they deserve. Generative AI changes that by building item banks for areas like CTE and world languages, making these subjects more engaging and effective.
### Streamlineand Personalize Formative Assessments
Formative assessments are crucial for understanding where students stand but can be labor-intensive. With AI, teachers can efficiently group students by their needs and design individualized assessments pinpointing learning gaps.
With[Performance Matters AI](https://www.powerschool.com/powerschool-ai/powerbuddy/powerbuddy-for-assessment/) capabilities, educators can:
\* \*\*Create single questions:\*\* Quickly generate a multiple-choice question aligned with standards, grade levels, and topics to review and customize before adding to assessments.
\* \*\*Bulk-create content:\*\* Generate multiple questions at once for a standard and save them to a shared item bank for an entire school or district
\* \*\*Build unique content:\*\* Craft fictional and non-fictional passages, stories, or explanatory texts for assessments. Create multiple versions of content at different reading levels to support all learners.
\* \*\*Design full assessments fast:\*\* Generate and organize items for a complete assessment, perfect for tailoring to different student groups or to provide “just-in-time” teaching on challenging topics.
### Gain Insights with Standards Mastery Reporting
The new standards mastery reporting adds another layer of insight, helping teachers make data-informed decisions. By removing outdated dependencies, these tools now provide actionable feedback for a broader range of students, empowering teachers to adjust instruction and drive results. Now, teachers can see standards results by test, standards results by students, or standards results across multiple assessments or student groups.
## Generative AI Simplifies Content Creation in Schoology Learning
Generative AI in PowerSchool [Schoology Learning](https://www.powerschool.com/classroom/schoology-learning/) takes the guesswork out of content creation, giving teachers an easy, AI-powered way to enhance assignments and discussions. Educators can craft engaging material with the Generate Content feature by providing a few inputs. The content is ready to use instantly, saving them valuable time and effort.
Additionally, Math Logic Advancements offers enhanced support for students, providing detailed, step-by-step explanations to improve their understanding of complex math concepts.
Now, it’s easier than ever to create tailored resources, engage students, and meet diverse learning needs—all within a single platform. Here’s a closer look at the tools available in Schoology Learning.
This seamless integration of AI in Schoology Learning empowers educators to focus on teaching while letting the tools handle the heavy lifting of content creation. Simplifying repetitive tasks ensures that every assignment or discussion is engaging, relevant, and tailored to students’ needs.
While Schoology streamlines lesson creation and engagement, PowerBuddy builds on this foundation by offering students personalized, one-on-one support.
## PowerBuddyTransforms Student Learning in Schoology
We are excited to introduce[PowerBuddy for Learning](https://www.powerschool.com/powerschool-ai/powerbuddy/powerbuddy-for-learning/), the safe and secure AI assistant redefining how students learn in Schoology Learning. Designed specifically for K-12 education, PowerBuddy gives students instant, one-on-one support with their assignments while creating personalized learning pathways. 
### How PowerBuddy Supports Students
\* \*\*On-demand guidance:\*\* Whether they’re stuck on a problem or need clarification, PowerBuddy provides immediate, tailored help.
\* \*\*Resources at their fingertips:\*\* Students can access everything they need to succeed, from detailed explanations to study tips.
\* \*\*A personalized learning journey:\*\* PowerBuddy adapts to each student’s needs, making traditional learning more engaging and effective.
![]()
PowerBuddy‘s Socratic Mode encourages deeper learning by prompting critical thinking without giving away answers. Research Mode supports open-ended exploration, making it ideal for project-based or inquiry-driven work.
### Ensure Transparency and Inclusivity
All student interactions with PowerBuddy are logged and monitored for sensitive topics, providing transparency to educators and families. Schoology Learning now supports Arabic and Thai languages, including right-to-left writing, expanding access for a broader range of students.
### Access District Information Anytime
PowerBuddy also answers questions about district resources, including policy manuals, athletic schedules, after-school programs, student handbooks, district calendars, lunch menus, and job postings.
Built with [responsible AI principles](https://www.powerschool.com/blog/the-ethics-of-ai-in-edtech/), PowerBuddy ensures every interaction is secure, meaningful, and designed to support student success. It‘s like having a trusted tutor available anytime, ready to help students gain confidence and take their learning to the next level.
## Streamline Curriculum Planning with Enhanced Tools
PowerSchool [Curriculum & Instruction](https://www.powerschool.com/classroom/curriculum-and-instruction/) streamlines lesson planning and curriculum mapping with these 2025 updates, designed to save time and make alignment easier for administrators and educators.
### Replace Off-Days Tab with Academic Terms Tab
The new Academic Terms Tab gives administrators a centralized way to manage academic terms, replacing the old “Days Off“ feature. By aligning curriculum maps to instructional days within a grading term, schools can stay better organized and adapt to their schedules more effectively.
### Improve Pacing Tab on Curriculum Maps
Historically, creating pacing guides that reflect what’s happening in the classroom has been a challenge. Unit durations, based on lesson counts rather than time, left no room for gaps or overlaps. Off days affected all schools equally, regardless of individual schedules. Now, the new pacing guide improvements put school calendars front and center. Curriculum writers can schedule units with accurate dates and adjust pacing easily using an intuitive interface.
Pacing guides now also include a more dynamic and interactive interface. Assigning Academic Terms to curriculum maps unlocks date fields on units and introduces a Gantt chart view for better visibility across classrooms, leadership, and central office.
### Bulk Assign Academic Term
Save time by applying academic terms to multiple curriculum maps or schools at once. Administrators can push essential events and custom schedules across maps using the “Bulk Assign“ workflow, ensuring everyone is ready to hit the ground running each year. This bulk assignment feature removes the need to edit maps individually, making it easier for administrators to manage district-wide schedules.
## ContentNav and PowerBuddy Elevate Personalized Learning
Finding approved content can often feel like searching for a needle in a haystack. Educators struggle with disparate resources scattered across direct links, videos, and other platforms and content that lacks proper tagging for standards, making it hard to find and use efficiently.
PowerSchool [ContentNav](https://www.powerschool.com/classroom/contentnav/) offers a powerful [AI-powered learning object repository for K-12 education](https://www.powerschool.com/blog/introducing-powerschool-content-nav/) with district-approved, standards-aligned instructional content in one location. ContentNav continues to evolve with robust tools to support K-12 education by providing district-approved, standards-aligned resources all in one place.
With millions of disaggregated content pieces from various trusted vendors, ContentNav allows teachers to:
\* Search by metadata, such as standard, grade, subject, and content type.
\* Access district-approved resources seamlessly embedded within Schoology Learning.
\* Select, assign, and easily deploy content directly into assignments or courses.
## Create Content Faster with PowerBuddy for Content
PowerBuddy for Content lives within ContentNav, offering fast, AI-powered content creation. This tool is fine-tuned specifically for K-12 education, ensuring safe and effective content generation that benefits both educators and students. Educators can now provide feedback on created content, driving continuous improvement and ensuring better resources for the entire community.
### What can PowerBuddy for Content do?
\* \*\*Fill content gaps:\*\* Generate resources for under-resourced areas or subject gaps.
\* \*\*Create customized content:\*\* Quickly produce passages, stories, assignments, or model texts tailored by length, reading level, or language.
\* \*\*Enable personalized learning:\*\* Generate multiple versions of the same content to accommodate diverse learner needs.
\* \*\*Seamlessly integrate into workflows:\*\* ContentNav’s native integration with Schoology Learning means educators can create and deploy content without leaving their existing system.
## Empower Educators and Students with 2025 Enhancements
The 2025 enhancements to PowerSchool personalized learning solutions deliver impactful tools to educators, students, and administrators. With generative AI integrated into Schoology Learning, Performance Matters, Curriculum & Instruction, and ContentNav, teachers now have everything they need to create truly personalized learning experiences.
These innovations can help simplify workflows, save time, and empower educators to focus on what matters most: supporting every student’s unique journey.
