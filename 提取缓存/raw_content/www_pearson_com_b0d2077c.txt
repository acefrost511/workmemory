7 AI tools to explore with your students in 2025
================================================


January 21, 2025
by


![]()

AI has already made education more engaging, innovative, and impactful. Step into the future with these AI tools for your classroom this year.

Artificial intelligence is slowly losing its novelty as it becomes integrated into our day to day lives. These days, [more than half](https://www.forbes.com/advisor/education/it-and-tech/artificial-intelligence-in-school/) of teachers agree that AI positively impacts educational outcomes, with three out of five teachers using the technology in their classrooms last year. AI can help teachers to create lesson plans, materials and assessments, as well as streamlining administrative tasks.

Meanwhile, students are turning to AI to help improve their writing skills, summarise information, and get feedback on assignments. 73% of students [say AI helps them](https://quizlet-web.cdn.prismic.io/quizlet-web/9aed71f9-41e9-4dd8-bf6e-7f1cb49f519e_State+of+AI+in+Education_Infographic.pdf) generally, and 67% say they study more efficiently with the help of AI.

So, it’s safe to assume that AI integration will continue in 2025. What’s important is that both educators and students know how to navigate the most up-to-date tools in order to enhance the learning process.

Here’s our list of the top AI tools to add to your toolbox in 2025.

AI tools for schoolwork
-----------------------

These AI resources can help you design and deliver schoolwork, giving your students more personalised and engaging learning experiences – and save you time!

### Khanmigo

[Khanmigo](https://www.khanmigo.ai/teachers) is an AI-powered assistant that helps teachers create dynamic and fun lessons for students. The app is free for educators in [40+ countries](https://blog.khanacademy.org/teachers-in-44-countries-now-get-free-access-to-khanmigo-in-english-thanks-to-microsoft-support/) and is used by over 6 million teachers globally.

Khanmigo is a great tool for coming up with unique lesson plans, quiz questions, in-class activities, discussion prompts, and lesson hooks. It even provides an answer key with explanations.

You’ll notice a dramatic difference in the time you spend lesson planning, allowing you to spend more time interacting with students.

### EdPuzzle

Have you ever wished you could make educational videos more interactive in the classroom? [EdPuzzle](https://edpuzzle.com/) lets you do just that.

Easily import videos from sources such as YouTube, and then embed quiz questions, voiceovers, and other interactive elements directly into them. You’re able to share videos and resources with your colleagues, and there’s even a Student Projects feature which allows your students to get creative and make their own videos!

### Canva for Education

With [Canva for Education](https://www.canva.com/education/), you’ll no longer need to spend hours creating visually engaging materials that capture your students’ attention. This AI-powered design tool provides you with a whole library of resources to tap into, enabling you to quickly craft captivating slides, posters, worksheets, and videos.

Students can also use Canva to complete their assignments, and you can share feedback directly through the platform or link it to your learning management system. Better yet, Canva is completely free for primary and secondary educators and available worldwide.

### Rosebud.ai

If your students enjoy playing games in class, they will love [Rosebud.ai](https://www.rosebud.ai/education) - and they’ll learn something new in the process. All you have to do is describe the game you want to play in the AI-powered chat and it will write the code and create it for you.

You can continue to tweak and add new features to your educational game as you try it out, and add AI characters that act as teaching assistants. These AI-powered games are fun and engaging for your students, and allow you to use narrative experiences to explain complex themes in a more digestible way.

AI tools for homework
---------------------

While you might think that [AI is a recipe for cheating](https://www.pearson.com/international-schools/international-schools-blog/2024/12/5-ways-to-discourage-your-students-from-cheating-with-ai-.html) when students use it for homework, there are innovative tools that teach students to use AI as a research assistant and study support. Here are our favourites for 2025.

### Wordsuperb

Using AI to help with essay writing doesn’t have to mean getting ChatGPT to do it for you. [Wordsuperb](https://wordsuperb.com/home) streamlines the writing process, helping students craft the perfect title, create structured outlines, and find credible sources to support their arguments.

It also corrects grammar and spelling mistakes and polishes writing style, enabling students to focus their energies on the creative aspects of writing essays and constructing convincing arguments.

### Quizlet

[Quizlet](https://quizlet.com/) has been around for a while, but it’s now enhanced with AI and goes much further than digital flashcards. Students can use Quizlet to turn study materials like slides, videos, and notes into flashcard sets that they study within the app. The app also generates personalised practice tests and revision sessions to get them well-prepared for exam season.

Quizlet also now features an in-app AI tutor, [Q-Chat](https://quizlet.com/qchat-personal-ai-tutor). Built using ChatGPT, Q-Chat provides tailored guidance and support to help students deepen their understanding of study material and feel more invested in their learning.

### Study Potion

Do your students feel the stress when exam season rolls around, and end up drowning themselves in reams of highlighted notes?

[Study Potion](https://www.studypotion.ai/) is a tool where students can generate personalised study materials from uploaded content, get notes and summaries from any document, and even ‘chat’ with study material to ask questions and clarify concepts.

Whether they’re struggling with a complex maths problem, memorizing vocabulary in a foreign language, or diving into literary themes, Study Potion acts as a powerful homework helper and study tool.

AI in 2025 and beyond
---------------------

By learning how AI tools can fuel smart learning, both students and teachers benefit. Students are better positioned to succeed in an AI-led world, and teachers can streamline admin and planning, allowing them to focus more on student interaction.
