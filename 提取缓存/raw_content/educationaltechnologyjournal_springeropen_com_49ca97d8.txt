Overview
--------

Publish your research in the International Journal of Educational Technology in Higher Education. This peer-reviewed journal features current research in Educational Technology within Higher Education. Make sure to review the journal's [aims and scope](https://link.springer.com/journal/41239/aims-and-scope) carefully before submitting your research.
