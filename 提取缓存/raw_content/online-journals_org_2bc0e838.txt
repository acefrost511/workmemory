## About the Journal
This interdisciplinary journal aims to focus on the exchange of relevant trends and research results as well as the presentation of practical experiences gained while developing and testing elements of technology enhanced learning in post-secondary and secondary education. So it aims to bridge the gap between pure academic research journals and more practical publications. So it covers the full range from research, application development to experience reports and product descriptions.
\*\*iJET is an Open Access Journal.\*\* Online readers don't have to pay any fee.
However, a subscription to \*\*printed copies\*\* is available. Please fill in and follow the instructions in this [PDF Form](https://www.akinik.com/SubscriptionForm.pdf).
