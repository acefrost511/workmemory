**标题**：Artificial Intelligence Literacy of Primary and Secondary School Students: Connotation Evolution, Target Upgrade and 3×3 Framework Construction

**卷期**：2026年第3期（第36卷，第299期）

**英文摘要**：

This study examines the connotation evolution and target upgrade of artificial intelligence literacy among primary and secondary school students. Based on an analysis of the changing demands for AI literacy in the intelligent era, the study proposes a 3×3 framework for K-12 AI literacy education, systematically delineating the levels, dimensions, and competency indicators of AI literacy that primary and secondary school students should develop. The research provides theoretical guidance and practical reference for the construction of AI literacy education in basic education.
