## 摘要

The digital transformation of K12 schools has become an important way to improve the quality of education, optimize the allocation of educational resources, and promote educational equity. The process and path of school digital transformation is complex and proactive, and it is increasingly important to explore the path of digital transformation in K12 schools under differentiated contexts. In this study, from the perspective of organizational change, we constructed the dynamic mechanism of K12 school digital transformation with four dimensions, namely, vision, technology, personnel and environment, and based on 75 good digital school practice cases, we used the fuzzy set qualitative comparative analysis method to explore the dynamic linkage effect among multiple factors affecting K12 school digital transformation from a group perspective. The study finds that there are three feasible paths for digital transformation of K12 schools, such as "personnel-led", "multifaceted synergistic", and "environmentally forced", and puts forward feasible solutions for the digital transformation of schools, which are universally applicable and instructive.

K12学校数字化转型已成为提升教育质量、优化教育资源配置、促进教育公平的重要途径。学校数字化转型过程与路径具有复杂性、主动性，探究差异化情境下K12学校数字化转型路径日益重要。该文从组织变革视角出发，构建包括愿景、技术、人员、环境四个维度的K12学校数字化转型动力机制，基于75所数字化好学校实践案例，运用模糊集定性比较分析方法（fsQCA），从组态视角探究影响K12学校数字化转型的多要素联动效应。研究发现，K12学校数字化转型存在三条可行路径。

# 核心发现

### 三条可行数字化转型路径（fsQCA组态分析）
1. **人员主导型路径**：以人员要素为核心驱动力的数字化转型
2. **多元协同型路径**：多要素协同联动推进转型
3. **环境倒逼型路径**：外部环境压力驱动转型

### 研究方法
- 模糊集定性比较分析（fsQCA）
- 75所数字化好学校实践案例样本
- 四维度分析框架：愿景、技术、人员、环境

### 研究意义
- 为差异化情境下的K12学校数字化转型提供路径参考
- 揭示多要素联动效应机制
- 提出具有普适性和指导意义的学校数字化转型解决方案
