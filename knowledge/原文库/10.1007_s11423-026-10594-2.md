# 核心发现

**背景：** 计算机视觉（Computer Vision）是计算机算法对视觉图像的自动分析，包括目标检测与识别、三维形状估计、材质识别和分割。

**干预：** 为期2-3周的专业发展项目，面向亚利桑那州和乔治亚州Title I学校/学区的初中教师，每站点培训6名在职教师。

**研究问题：**
- 教师对计算机视觉的认知如何变化？
- 对人类视觉的认知如何变化？
- 对计算机视觉与人类视觉的认知差异？

**主要发现：**
- 教师对计算机视觉的认知发生变化，所有4个树状图（含1-8个构念）内的构念转移
- 人类视觉认知在各构念簇中相对一致
- 人类视觉构念簇成员变动6%（n=1）
- 计算机视觉构念簇变动72%（n=13）
- 较短的专业发展体验（2-3周）即可对在职教师的计算机视觉课堂使用认知产生影响

## 摘要
Computer vision is the automated analysis of visual imagery by computer algorithms that includes, but not limited to object detection and identification, three-dimensional shape estimation, material recognition, and segmentation. The intervention consisted of two to three weeks of professional development that emphasized computer vision technologies with middle school teachers from Title I schools/districts in the states of Arizona and Georgia. Each location trained six in-service teachers. Results showed that in-service teachers' perspectives of computer vision changed with construct shifts within all four dendrograms; all clusters yielded mean increases. Perspectives of human vision stayed relatively consistent across two clusters. The significance of this study rests in its contribution to the limited research on computer vision in teacher education. The results show that a relatively short professional development experience can have an impact on in-service teachers' perspectives of computer vision classroom use.
