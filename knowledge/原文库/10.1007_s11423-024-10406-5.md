## 摘要
This research addresses a critical gap in teacher preparation by presenting the "four pedagogies for developing computational thinking" (4P4CT) framework, designed specifically for implementation in a computational thinking course for all preservice K-12 teachers. The study investigates how this comprehensive framework can be effectively integrated into teacher education programs to equip future educators with the necessary computational thinking competencies to teach across all K-12 grade levels and subject domains. Through a systematic approach, the authors developed and implemented course materials that incorporate four distinct pedagogical approaches: constructionism, context-based learning, project-based learning, and problem-based learning, each carefully designed to foster computational thinking skills in preservice teachers from diverse disciplinary backgrounds.

# 核心发现
本研究为职前K-12教师设计并实施了一门计算思维课程，采用"发展计算思维的四种教学法"（4P4CT）框架。该框架包含四个教学维度：概念理解（Perspective）、实践应用（Practice）、教学设计（Pedagogy）和评估反思（Performance）。研究在多所师范院校开展，评估了职前教师在完成课程后计算思维能力的提升效果。研究发现4P4CT框架能有效提升职前教师的计算思维水平，且教师在课程中获得的亲身学习体验对其未来课堂教学设计有积极影响。研究为K-12教师计算思维培养提供了可复制、可推广的课程模式，对教师教育课程改革具有重要参考价值。
