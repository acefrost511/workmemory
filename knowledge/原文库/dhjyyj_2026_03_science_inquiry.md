**标题**：Generative Artificial Intelligence Enabling Scientific Inquiry-based Learning: Value Connotation, Practical Framework and Implementation Strategies

**期刊**：电化教育研究

**卷期**：2026年第3期

**作者**：郭利明，汪灶文，郑勤华

**关键词**：生成式人工智能；科学探究学习；科学教育；智能体；拔尖创新人才

**英文摘要**：

In the reform of science education in the new era, students' scientific inquiry abilities have become a key concern, calling for its cultivation through inquiry-based learning in primary and secondary schools. However, students' scientific inquiry-based learning faces numerous challenges in practice: inadequate reflection of competency goals, pseudo-inquiry tasks, lack of autonomy and adaptive support in the inquiry process, and a disconnect in dynamic assessment mechanisms for learning-evaluation feedback. The remarkable development of generative artificial intelligence presents new opportunities for overcoming these challenges. Therefore, from the perspective of the enabling role of generative artificial intelligence, the study analyzes its core value in inquiry practices, competency development, and talent cultivation it brings to students' science inquiry-based learning, and constructs a practical framework centered on problem-solving and value realization, comprising core drivers, generative artificial intelligence-driven 5E scientific inquiry-based learning, and a multidirectional value exchange system linking learning, competency, and talent development. Finally, the study proposes the following implementation strategies: developing specialized intelligent agents for scientific inquiry-based learning, emphasizing the relationship of teacher-machine-student, enhancing students' AI literacy, strengthening students' scientific identity, and remaining vigilant against the risk of alienation from technology dependency among teachers and students. The study theoretically elucidates the fundamental developmental trajectory of inquiry-based learning in the intelligent era, offering insights for advancing inquiry-based learning in primary and secondary schools and contributing to the high-quality development of science education.
