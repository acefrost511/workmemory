## 摘要

This study evaluates the effectiveness of an AI model in facilitating cross-linguistic language learning through a randomized controlled trial (RCT). Cross-linguistic language learning refers to the process of leveraging knowledge from one language to facilitate acquisition of another. The RCT design provides rigorous causal evidence on AI tutoring efficacy compared to control conditions. The study examines learner outcomes across multiple linguistic dimensions, including vocabulary acquisition, grammatical competence, and cross-linguistic transfer, in the context of AI-mediated language instruction. Findings from the RCT contribute to evidence-based understanding of how AI models can be effectively deployed to support cross-linguistic learning processes.
