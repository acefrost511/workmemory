## 摘要

Teacher education students play double role as present learners and future educators. Hence, they need targeted AI literacy training to navigate the rapidly evolving AI landscape. This study examines how a structured AI literacy programme influences preservice teachers' AI knowledge, attitudes and self-efficacy. Results indicate significant improvements in all three domains, with preservice teachers demonstrating enhanced capacity to critically evaluate AI tools and integrate them pedagogically. The programme's emphasis on hands-on AI tool exploration and reflection was particularly effective.
