## 摘要

This editorial introduces a special issue exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and K-12 education. The article discusses the transformative impact of generative AI tools like ChatGPT (launched November 30, 2022) on school education, examining both the possibilities and challenges of AI in educational contexts. The authors provide a critical overview of AI in Education (AI&ED), covering its history, applications (student-focused, teacher-focused, and institution-focused), and ethical concerns. Key topics include: the distinction between generative AI and broader AI&ED research; adaptive tutoring systems; personalized learning claims; privacy and surveillance issues; bias in AI systems; commercial exploitation of student data; and the impact on teacher agency and the teaching profession. The editorial emphasizes the need for critical reflection on AI's role in education, highlighting risks such as homogenization of students, reductionist views of education, and the erosion of human connection in learning.
