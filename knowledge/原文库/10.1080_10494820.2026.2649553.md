Abstract:
This study uses an international Delphi approach to identify consensus on central components of AI literacy for K–12 education. The research involved expert panels across multiple countries working toward consensus on what constitutes AI literacy in K-12 contexts. The results align with other recent AI literacy frameworks, contributing to the global conversation on defining and operationalizing AI literacy for school education. The study addresses a critical gap: unlike adult or higher education contexts where AI literacy frameworks are relatively well-developed, K-12 education lacks consensus on what knowledge, skills, and dispositions students should develop regarding AI. By bringing together international experts through iterative Delphi rounds, this research aims to build a shared understanding of the essential components of AI literacy for young learners, including technical understanding, critical evaluation, ethical awareness, and practical application skills.

## 摘要

本研究采用国际德尔菲法（Delphi），旨在识别K-12教育阶段人工智能素养（AI Literacy）的核心组成要素并达成国际共识。研究汇聚多国专家小组，通过多轮迭代问卷，识别出K-12阶段AI素养的关键维度，包括：技术理解、批判性评估、伦理意识和实际应用能力。与近年来其他AI素养框架一致，本研究为K-12阶段AI教育内容的界定提供了国际参考。
