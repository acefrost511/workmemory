## 摘要

Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools are becoming increasingly popular in education, but their integration into classroom practice remains challenging, particularly in literacy instruction at the elementary level. This study employs a co-design approach to integrate AI tools into literacy lessons for elementary classrooms. Teachers and researchers collaborated throughout the design process to ensure pedagogical appropriateness and practical feasibility. The co-designed AI-enhanced literacy activities were implemented and evaluated in real classroom settings. Findings indicate that students engaged more deeply with literacy tasks when supported by AI tools, and teachers reported that the co-design process was essential for ensuring the AI tools were aligned with curriculum goals and student needs. The study highlights the importance of teacher involvement in the design and implementation of AI-enhanced educational interventions.
