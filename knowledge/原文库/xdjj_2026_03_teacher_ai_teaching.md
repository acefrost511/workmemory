**标题**：Willingness Measurement and Behavior Analysis of AI Teaching for Primary and Secondary School Teachers: A Multi-dimensional Empirical Study Based on National Scope

**期刊**：现代教育技术

**卷期**：2026年第3期（第36卷，第299期）

**关键词**：中小学教师；人工智能教学；意愿测量；结构方程模型

**英文摘要**：

This study constructs a willingness-behavior model for AI teaching among primary and secondary school teachers. Based on 1,121 questionnaire data nationwide, using structural equation modeling and association rule mining, the study systematically analyzes the application willingness, influencing mechanisms, and behavioral characteristics of AI teaching among primary and secondary school teachers. The research findings reveal the key factors influencing teachers' willingness to adopt AI in teaching and the current state of their AI teaching behaviors.
