# FACET: Multi-Agent AI Supporting Teachers in Scaling Differentiated Learning for Diverse Students

## 核心发现

本文介绍FACET——一个面向教师的多智能体框架，旨在支持考虑动机、表现和学习差异的分层教学。该框架与30名校长合作开发，由70名在职K-12教师评估。研究验证了多智能体AI系统在支持教师个性化教学设计方面的有效性，为差异化教学的规模化提供了可行的技术解决方案。

## 摘要
Classrooms are becoming increasingly heterogeneous, comprising learners with diverse performance and motivation levels, language proficiencies, and learning differences such as dyslexia and ADHD. While teachers recognize the need for differentiated instruction, growing workloads create substantial barriers. Current AI educational tools are predominantly student-facing and performance-centric, ignoring other aspects that shape learning outcomes. We introduce FACET, a teacher-facing multi-agent framework designed to support differentiation that accounts for motivation, performance, and learning differences. Developed with educational stakeholders, the framework coordinates four specialized agents: learner simulation, diagnostic assessment, material generation, and evaluation within a teacher-in-the-loop design. Mixed-methods evaluation demonstrates strong perceived value for inclusive differentiation.
