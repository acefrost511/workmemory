## 核心发现

为应对K-12学校教育技术相关的网络安全威胁上升及网络安全教育缺口，研究者设计并实施了基于多媒体的Edtech网络安全与隐私意识-提问-行动（AAA）专业发展项目，面向学校管理人员、技术支持人员和教师。

**主要发现：**
- 50名来自不同背景（学区类型和学校层次）的K-12学校人员参与
- Wilcoxon符号秩检验显示参与者知识在前后测间显著增长（V=265.50, P=0.012）
- 前测均值：9.02；后测均值：10.48
- 无技术背景参与者知识显著提升（V=116.00, P=0.016），而有技术背景者未显著提升（V=28.50, P=0.247）
- 郊区参与者知识显著提升（V=25.50, P=0.005），城市和农村参与者未显著
- 参与者在网络安全意识和识别网络威胁能力方面表达整体积极态度
- 未来需求：AI相关网络安全主题、真实世界案例的更多情境指导
- 资金来源：美国国家科学基金会（NSF），奖号#2309400

## 摘要
To address the rising cybersecurity threats associated with educational technology in K-12 schools and the gap in cybersecurity education, we designed and implemented a multimedia-based Educational Technology (Edtech) Cybersecurity and Privacy Awareness-Ask-Action-Accommodate (AAA) professional development (PD) for school administrators, technology support staff, and teachers. This study sought to identify the PD's effectiveness, specifically focusing on the participants' knowledge increments and the perceptions of their experience. Fifty K-12 school personnel with diverse demographic, technology and contextual backgrounds participated. Wilcoxon signed-rank tests indicated a significant increase in participants' knowledge between pre- and post-test. Participants expressed an overall positive attitude, appreciating the increased cybersecurity awareness and enhanced ability to recognize and act against potential cyber threats to protect students and schools.
