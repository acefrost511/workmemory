**标题**：Integration of Artificial Intelligence into Basic Education: Boosting Involution or Enhancing New Quality?

**期刊**：开放教育研究

**卷期**：2025年第31卷第2期

**作者**：胡小勇，刘雪旎，陈丽诗，刘颜帆，刘晓红

**关键词**：人工智能；基础教育；新质生产力；教育内卷

**英文摘要**：

Artificial intelligence as a new quality productive force is profoundly reshaping the educational landscape of basic education. On one hand, AI is regarded as an important driving force for basic education innovation; basic education is the cornerstone of building an education powerhouse. The integration of AI may either promote new quality development in education or contribute to educational involution, presenting a dual effect. AI is promoting comprehensive reform in basic education from aspects including curriculum system, teaching resources, teaching environment, teaching and evaluation, and teacher supply, helping to build a new ecosystem for talent cultivation.
