## 摘要

This comparative study investigates the role of generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) in enhancing feedback quality for digital multimodal compositions in language learning contexts. Digital multimodal compositions refer to language learning outputs that integrate multiple modes—including text, images, audio, and video. The study compares GenAI-generated feedback with traditional feedback approaches, evaluating effectiveness across dimensions including accuracy, specificity, learner uptake, and multimodal responsiveness. Findings reveal significant differences in feedback quality and learning outcomes, providing empirical evidence for the integration of GenAI in formative assessment practices for multimodal language tasks.
