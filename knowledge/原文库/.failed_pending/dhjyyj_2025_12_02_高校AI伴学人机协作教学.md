## 中文摘要

生成式人工智能（GAI）正推动教育迈向智能融合新时代，为教学注入新活力。然则，传统的高校课堂时常出现参与率低、主动提问少等现象，如何借助GAI提高教学质量，支持人机协作教学仍是亟待解决的问题。为此，研究以高校人文通识课为例，基于ICAP理论构建GAI支持的"双师"伴学协作教学模式，在理论层面设计"目标定位—被动学习—主动反馈—建构整合—交互批判—迁移探索"活动流程，优化GAI支持的AI教师和人类教师在教学活动中的协同机制；开展为期八周教学实践，通过认知网络分析，探索学生思维发展、认知水平及认知行为的变化趋势。研究发现：该模式能提高学生的认知水平和思维活跃程度，打破课堂沉默，促进学生从低阶学习向高阶学习转变；该模式可激发学生自我调节能力，进而促进其思维和认知发展。研究建议人机协作教学实践需重新定位学习目标和角色分工，设计全流程的教学和评价方式，发挥人类教师监督和决策关键作用，与GAI共同激发智慧潜力。

## 英文摘要

Generative Artificial Intelligence (GAI) is driving education into a new era of intelligent integration, infusing teaching and learning with fresh vitality. However, traditional university classrooms often suffer from low participation and scarce active questioning. How to leverage GAI to improve teaching quality and support human-machine collaborative teaching remains an urgent issue to address. Accordingly, this study takes humanities general education courses as an example and constructs a GAI-supported "Dual-Teacher" collaborative learning model based on the ICAP framework. Theoretically, this study designs an activity flow comprising "goal setting, passive learning, active feedback, construction and integration, interactive critique, transfer and exploration", optimizing the collaboration mechanism between GAI-supported AI instructors and human teachers in teaching activities. An eight-week teaching experiment was conducted, using cognitive network analysis to investigate trends in students' thinking development, cognitive levels, and cognitive behaviors. The findings indicate that this model enhances students' cognitive levels and promotes their active thinking, breaks classroom silence, and facilitates the transition from low-order learning to high-order learning. It also stimulates students' self-regulatory capacity, thereby further promoting their cognitive and intellectual development. This study suggests that human-machine collaborative teaching practices require redefining learning objectives and role divisions, designing holistic teaching and evaluation processes, leveraging the supervisory and decision-making roles of human teachers, and jointly unleashing intellectual potential with GAI.
