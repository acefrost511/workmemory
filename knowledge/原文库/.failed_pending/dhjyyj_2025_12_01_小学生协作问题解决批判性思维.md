# 生成式人工智能支持的协作问题解决对小学生批判性思维的影响研究

## 中文摘要

协作问题解决被认为是培养学生批判性思维的一种有效途径。然而，小学生的知识局限常阻碍其群体间产生深度、有效的对话，影响协作问题解决对批判性思维的支撑效果，生成式人工智能为解决这一难题带来契机。研究采用行动研究法，在小学五年级科学课堂中，经过三轮迭代，探索以豆包为代表的生成式人工智能工具在小学生协作问题解决活动中的作用方式及其对批判性思维发展的作用效果。结果表明，相较于人工智能"独立介入"而言，"师机协同"介入方式能更有效地提升小学生协作问题解决的质量，进而促进其批判性思维的发展。文章最后尝试构建师机协同支持下的CPS作用过程与机制，并从学习发生的条件和元认知视角探讨了生成式人工智能介入CPS后提升学生批判性思维发展的可能原因。

## 英文摘要

Collaborative problem-solving is considered an effective approach to fostering students' critical thinking skills. However, the limited knowledge of elementary school students often hinders the generation of deep, effective dialog among groups, thereby undermining the supportive role of collaborative problem-solving in fostering critical thinking. Generative artificial intelligence presents an opportunity to address this challenge. This study employed action research methodology, conducting three rounds of iterative exploration in fifth-grade science classrooms to investigate the role of generative AI tools, represented by Doubao, in collaborative problem-solving activities among elementary school students and their impact on the development of critical thinking. The results indicate that compared to AI "independent intervention", the "teacher-machine collaboration" intervention approach is more effective in enhancing the quality of collaborative problem-solving among elementary school students, thereby promoting the development of their critical thinking. Finally, this paper attempts to construct the action process and mechanism of CPS under the support of teacher-machine collaboration, and explores the potential reasons for enhancing the development of students' critical thinking through generative AI intervention in CPS, from the perspectives of conditions for learning occurrence and metacognition.
