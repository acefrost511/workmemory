# AI in Education Beyond Learning Outcomes: Cognition, Agency, Emotion, and Ethics

## 基本信息
## 摘要
Artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly being integrated into educational contexts, promising personalized support and increased efficiency. However, growing evidence suggests that the uncritical adoption of AI may produce unintended harms that extend beyond individual learning outcomes to affect broader societal goals. This paper examines the societal implications of AI in education through an integrative framework with four interrelated dimensions: cognition, agency, emotional well-being, and ethics. We synthesize existing evidence to show how AI-driven cognitive offloading, diminished learner agency, emotional disengagement, and surveillance-oriented practices can mutually reinforce one another. We argue that these dynamics risk undermining critical thinking, intellectual autonomy, emotional resilience, and trust, capacities that are foundational both for effective learning and also for democratic participation and informed civic engagement.

## 核心发现

### 研究概述

人工智能正在迅速整合到教育环境中，承诺个性化支持和提高效率。然而，越来越多的证据表明，不加批判地采用AI可能产生超出个人学习成果的意外伤害，影响更广泛的社会目标。

### 整合框架：四个相互关联的维度

1. **认知（Cognition）**
2. **代理（Agency）**
3. **情感健康（Emotional Well-being）**
4. **伦理（Ethics）**

### 主要发现

#### AI驱动的风险相互强化

研究从教育、认知科学、心理学和伦理学的角度综合现有证据，表明：

- AI驱动的认知卸载
- 学习者代理的削弱
- 情感脱离
- 以监控为导向的做法

这些动态可能相互强化，破坏：
- 批判性思维
- 智力自主
- 情感韧性
- 信任

这些能力既是有效学习的基础，也是民主参与和知情公民参与的基础。

### AI影响的条件性

AI的影响取决于设计和治理：
- ** pedagogically aligned**：符合教学原则
- **ethically grounded**：伦理基础
- **human-centered**：以人为中心

这些AI系统可以：
- 支持努力推理的脚手架
- 支持学习者代理
- 保留有意义的社交互动

### 研究结论

核心挑战不是AI是否应该用于教育，而是如何设计和治理AI以支持学习，同时维护教育的社会和公民目的。
