## 摘要
Rapid developments in artificial intelligence (AI) require dynamic adaptation in education to integrate new technologies timely and sustainably. In particular, the rise of generative AI requires leadership to implement it in a meaningful way for teaching and learning. School leaders have a special role to play in driving digital transformation. Based on a sample of German school leaders, this article explores how school leadership approaches and a digital mindset influence the implementation of AI in schools. Our findings provide initial and preliminary evidence that school leaders' digital mindsets, particularly proactive agility and empathy, understood as perspective taking, influence the implementation of AI in schools. Furthermore, the findings highlight the effectiveness of ambidextrous leadership in driving AI implementation.

# 核心发现
本研究探讨了学校领导者（校长）在AI转型中的关键作用，强调"数字思维"是推动学校AI转型的首要条件。研究通过对多所学校校长进行深度访谈和案例分析，揭示了校长数字思维与学校AI采纳程度之间的正相关关系。研究发现具备数字思维的校长更能有效推动教师专业发展、制定AI教育政策、营造创新文化。研究者提出了校长数字思维的核心构成要素，包括技术乐观主义、持续学习意愿、跨学科整合能力和数据驱动决策思维。研究为学校管理层AI能力建设提供了理论依据和实践路径，对教育行政部门的校长培训政策具有重要启示。
