This study develops and tests an NLU (Natural Language Understanding)-integrated smart diagnostic branched tree platform for AI-assisted formative assessment in social studies education. The platform combines AI diagnostic capabilities with branched tree assessment methodology to provide personalized feedback and identify students' misconceptions in real time. The researchers developed the platform, implemented it in social studies classrooms, and tested its effects on student learning outcomes. The study contributes to the growing body of research on AI-powered educational technology by demonstrating how natural language understanding can enhance formative assessment practices in K-12 social studies contexts.

## 摘要

本研究开发并测试了一款基于自然语言理解（NLU）技术的智能诊断分支树平台，用于社会研究课程的AI辅助形成性评价。该平台将AI诊断能力与分支树评估方法相结合，实时提供个性化反馈，精准识别学生误解。研究团队开发了该平台，在社会研究课堂中实施并测试其对学生学习效果的影响。研究为AI驱动教育技术文献作出贡献，展示了自然语言理解技术如何提升K-12社会研究课程形成性评价实践的效果。
