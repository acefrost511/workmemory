# 核心发现

## 研究背景
K-12教育如何处理生成式人工智能（GAI）取决于教育工作者的准备程度和支持结构。本研究探讨教育专业人员对GAI的认知及其支持需求。

## 研究设计
- 研究方法：横断面设计，混合方法
- 参与者：来自K-12各年级和各类教育角色的452名教育专业人员
- 干预措施：围绕GAI在教育中的应用开展专业发展（PD）干预
- 理论框架：变革性学习理论（Transformative Learning Theory）
- 数据收集：投票、调查、同行反思

## 核心发现
1. **认知-行为差距**：仅有31%的参与者使用过AI聊天机器人，但88%愿意采用。存在对GAI的积极态度与实际使用信心之间的显著差距——研究揭示了热情与犹豫并存的现象。

2. 教育专业人员需要超越技术技能的综合专业发展和组织支持，以倡导负责任、可持续和合乎伦理的GAI使用。

3. 研究为政策制定者提供了实现全校范围GAI整合的洞见，强调设计创新视角可以指导这一过程。

## 结论
有效、可持续的K-12 GAI整合需要全面的政策支持、专业发展培训和超越技术技能的组织保障。本研究为K-12教育中负责任的GAI整合提供了实践指导。

## 摘要
How K-12 education will handle generative artificial intelligence (GAI) is dependent upon educators' preparedness and support structures. This study explores educational professionals' perceptions regarding GAI and their support needs. 452 educational professionals, representing all years levels and educational roles in K-12, participated in a professional development (PD) intervention focused on GAI in education. Drawing on this cross-sectional design we adopted a mixed-methods approach. Data from polls, surveys, and collegial reflections were analysed using descriptive statistics, thematic analysis and Transformative Learning Theory. One of the key findings revealed a notable gap between positive attitudes toward GAI and an actual confidence in using it: although only 31% had used AI chatbots, 88% were inclined to adopt them. These results signal simultaneous enthusiasm and hesitation, and emphasise the need for comprehensive PD and organisational support that extends beyond technical skills to advocate for responsible, sustainable and ethical GAI use.
