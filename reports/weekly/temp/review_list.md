1. **[1]** Enhancing design ideation: comparing AIGC-engaged and traditional brainstorming in educational contexts
   - 来源：Educational Technology Research and Development (ETRD)
   - 时间：2026-02-26
   - 摘要：Generating creative ideas is essential for designers, as creativity underpins all subsequent stages of the design process. This study investigates the impact of Artificial Intelligence Generated Content (AIGC) engagement on design ideation compared to traditional brainstorming methods in educational contexts. The research examines how AIGC tools influence the quantity, quality, and diversity of ideas generated by students, and explores the pedagogical implications for integrating these technologies into design education curricula.

2. **[2]** An empirical longitudinal study of AI integration in transforming teachers' pedagogical content knowledge: insights from language educators in rural China
   - 来源：Educational Technology Research and Development (ETRD)
   - 时间：2026-02-25
   - 摘要：This longitudinal study, grounded in Dynamic Systems Theory (DST), explores how language teachers' integration of AI tools evolves over an 18-week period, revealing AI adoption as a complex pedagogical transformation rather than a simple technology implementation. The research investigates the transformation of teachers' pedagogical content knowledge (PCK) through AI integration, providing insights from language educators in rural China. Findings indicate that successful AI integration requires substantial changes in teaching beliefs, practices, and the development of new technological pedagogical content knowledge.
   - 优先级：**高**
