Founder Memo

As parents, caregivers, and storytellers, we all want the same thing—to give our children a world filled with wonder, learning, and joy. But in today’s digital landscape, finding content that is both magical and meaningful can feel like a challenge. That’s why we created GIANT—to build a safe, trusted space where kids don’t just watch stories, they become a part of them. Here, curiosity leads the way, imagination has no limits, and every experience is designed to spark learning, connection, and lifelong memories. We’re on this journey together, shaping a future where screen time isn’t just passive entertainment, but an opportunity for growth and adventure.

Welcome to GIANT. Let’s create the nostalgia of tomorrow—together.
