### AI tutors for exam prep

Miyagi Labs is an AI-powered exam prep platform that helps students ace tests like the SAT and ACT through our AI tutor, personalized study plans, thousands of practice questions, and realistic practice exams.
We’re building the platform we wish we had when studying for exams. No more running out of practice questions. Track your progress as your rating in different exam categories increases, and know exactly what you need to practice most.

Hi everyone! We are [Guang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/guang-cui/) and [Tyrone](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tyroned3/) - cofounders of Miyagi Labs.

**TL;DR**

We use AI to transform YouTube content into complete, interactive courses—imagine a 3Blue1Brown video turned into an online course with quizzes and study material. We help people learn better with active practice and personalized feedback.

[Demo video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-8AfVemdtr0)

**The Problem**

Learning is flawed: online resources are everywhere, but 1-1 tutoring (which most people can’t afford) is still 2 standard deviations more effective than other methods.

YouTube has top-notch content on everything for free, but how do you turn that passive watching into active learning?

**Our Solution**

Content creators, educators, and learners can instantly convert educational videos into structured, interactive courses that drive real learning.

Our [platform](https://miyagilabs.ai/) automatically generates short-answer and multiple-choice questions, quizzes, and AI-driven feedback, turning passive watching into an active, engaging experience. Learners receive instant responses to their answers and can access an AI voice tutor for additional guidance.

Creators and educators, who love sharing knowledge but don’t have time to create entire courses, can focus on what they do best while we handle the active learning and engagement.

![]()

**The Team**

Guang and Tyrone met in college at MIT studying Computer Science and both are extremely passionate about learning and teaching.

Tyrone became a chess master in 4 years just from watching YouTube, has learned 4 languages, and has taught computer science around the world.

Guang has taught algorithms (as a TA), math, physics, and music to hundreds of students. He has also become an elite American Ninja Warrior and coached students at multiple gyms.

Because of our experiences, we have unique insights on how to learn and teach effectively.

![]()

**Our Ask**

If you know…

* Businesses that want to convert internal product videos or notes into online training courses for employees
* Educators or content creators who want to turn their videos into interactive courses
* Anyone interested in learning something new

…we'd love to hear from you.
