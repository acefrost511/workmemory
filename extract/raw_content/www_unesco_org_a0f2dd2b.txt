# Digital Learning Week
### The next edition of Digital Learning Week will take place on 7-11 September 2026 at UNESCO Headquarters in Paris
## About Digital Learning Week
Digital Learning Week is UNESCO's annual flagship event on ⁠[digital learning and the transformation of education](https://www.unesco.org/en/digital-education?hub=83250). Launched in 2023, the event convenes the community of digital education leaders, policy-makers, researchers and practitioners from various organizations, including UN agencies, governments, NGOs and the private sector, with a vision of steering technology for education.
UNESCO's Digital Learning Week presents a unique opportunity to participate in dynamic and thought-provoking discussions and engaging debates, co-create key resources and tools, and hear groundbreaking ideas and insights from world-renowned experts and leaders. The event aims to foster critical reflection and meaningful collaboration in advancing the ethical and human-centred digital transformation of education as we "reimagine our futures together."
