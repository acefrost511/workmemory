World Bank publishes report on AI in education
==============================================

26th July 20241 min read

The Artificial Intelligence revolution is rapidly reshaping education, offering unprecedented opportunities to personalise learning, support teachers and students, and improve educational management. The World Bank’s report, [*Artificial Intelligence Revolution in Education: What You Need to Know*,](https://www.worldbank.org/en/region/lac/publication/innovaciones-digitales-para-la-educacion-en-america-latina) highlights key AI-driven innovations and their practical applications for educators, students, parents, and institutions. As the first instalment in the *Digital Innovations in Education for Latin America and the Caribbean* series, this report aims to address the urgent learning crisis in the region by promoting the effective use of technology and digitalisation in education. The series seeks to equip students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in a changing educational landscape.
