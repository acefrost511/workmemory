27th International Conference on AI in Education

AIED 2026 · Seoul · Republic of Korea
=====================================

AIED 2026 will serve as a vibrant forum for researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to explore and shape
the evolving landscape of AI in education. Building on decades of innovation in the AIED community, this year’s
conference invites critical and creative examination of how emerging technologies, especially Generative AI, are
transforming the roles of both humans and AI in learning.

Conference theme

“From Tools To Teammates: Human-AI Synergy For Augmented Learning”
------------------------------------------------------------------

> “AIED is entering a new era in which AI must evolve from simply supporting human learning to enabling richer
> human AI collaboration.”

We welcome research on human and AI agency, collaborative intelligence, human learning and human & AI
co-evolving, especially work that reimagines AIED systems not as tools, but as adaptive teammates that
complement teachers and learners and help achieve shared educational goals.

[Please refer to the general Call for Papers for further details →](/2026/call-for-paper)



Why attend

Seoul evolves AIED for its 27th edition in 2026
-----------------------------------------------

Marking the 27th edition of the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Education,
AIED 2026 convenes at a moment when AI is reshaping learning, institutions, and societies at unprecedented
speed.
This year, we continue to examine emerging evidence, interrogate methodological and ethical challenges, and
explore the real-world impact of AI technologies in education.


Core research

### Advances in AI for learning

AIED 2026 highlights research on rapidly evolving AI technologies - from generative models to established
methods - and their role in augmenting learning as collaborative teammates.

[Explore research tracks →](/2026/call-for-paper)


BlueSky track

### Future directions for AIED

The BlueSky track invites visionary ideas that reimagine how AI may shape learning in the coming decades,
exploring
bold concepts beyond incremental improvements to today’s systems.

[BlueSky submission details →](/2026/call-for-paper/blue-sky)


WideAIED track

### Global and inclusive perspectives

WideAIED welcomes research addressing under-represented contexts and global challenges, focusing on equity,
accessibility, and AI systems that adapt to diverse social and technological environments.

[WideAIED submission details →](/2026/call-for-paper/wideaied)
