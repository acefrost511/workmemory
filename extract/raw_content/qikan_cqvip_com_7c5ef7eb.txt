# 人工智能与幼儿大脑教育开发 [认领](javascript:void(0))被引量：[3](javascript:void(0))
\*Artificial Intelligence and the Educational Development of Preschoolers'Brains\*
[引用](javascript:quoteDetail();)[收藏](javascript:void(0))[分享](javascript:void(0))

the re-establishment of the ability-based boundary between humans an...[MORE](javascript:void(0))\*\*As Artificial intelligence(AI)technologies led by large language models(LLMs)demonstrate astonishing emergent abilities,the world has been pushed into the age of intelligence.This is bound to result in the re-establishment of the ability-based boundary between humans and artificial tools,and the redefinition of the goals for the educational development of preschoolers'brains.In the educational development of preschoolers'brains in the age of intelligence,we need to respect the biological law of their development,develop the human brain's advantageous fields complementary to AI,adapt to the demands of social changes,and cultivate the ability in human-machine collaboration;and strengthen the educational development of preschoolers'brains to adapt to the social changes in the age of intelligence by means of the material environment that stimulates the senses,the spiritual atmosphere that reflects respect and acceptance,autonomous and free games,concrete and vivid language learning,and teaching paradigms by human-machine collaboration.We have noticed that LLMs open up an extensive and deep application space for AI;however,we should be clearly aware that LLM technology is essentially a probabilistic fitting system based on big data,and that in terms of energy consumption efficiency and cognitive depth,it lags behind the human brain by orders of magnitude.Therefore,it is difficult for it to help AI reach the level of human intelligence.If future AI wants to approach human capabilities,a more likely path is its learning from preschoolers'brains to develop the ability to build a world model through multimodal interactive perception.Therefore,a study of the educational development of preschoolers'brains is of great significance for a true breakthrough in Al technology.[FEWER](javascript:void(0))\*
