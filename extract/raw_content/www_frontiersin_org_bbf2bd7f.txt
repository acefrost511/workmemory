This article is a correction to:

2. Ethical and regulatory challenges of Generative AI in education: a systematic review
   
   2. [Read original article](/journals/education/articles/10.3389/feduc.2025.1565938/full)

A Correction on [Ethical and regulatory challenges of Generative AI in education: a systematic review](https://doi.org/10.3389/feduc.2025.1565938) by García-López, I. M., and Trujillo-Liñán, L. (2025). *Front. Educ*. 10:1565938. doi: [10.3389/feduc.2025.1565938](https://doi.org/10.3389/feduc.2025.1565938)

In the published article, there was an error in affiliation 2. Instead of “Institute of Humanities, Universidad Panamericana, Mexico City, Mexico” it should be “Universidad Panamericana, CDMX, Mexico”.

The original article has been updated.
