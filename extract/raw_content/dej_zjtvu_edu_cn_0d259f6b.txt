数智时代宏观教育学建构——基于教育结构性变革的审视
[3-10]
胡君进，黄晓磊
DOI:
[[DOI]10.15881/j.cnki.cn33-1304/g4.2026.02.001](http://dx.doi.org/[DOI]10.15881/j.cnki.cn33-1304/g4.2026.02.001)
摘要
摘要：
数智时代的到来正在深刻重塑教育的存在方式与整体结构。以技术工具论和人类中心主义为理论前提的传
统教育学，已难以解释技术从外在条件跃升为教育本体性结构后所引发的系统性变迁。基于技术本体论视角，数智
时代宏观教育学的核心任务，不在于回应具体教学技术的应用问题，而在于从整体层面把握教育结构正在发生的深
层重组。这一教育结构性变革集中体现在三个方面：其一，教育赖以运作的知识本体由以文本为中心的文化书写形
态，转向以数据与计算为基础的算法知识形态，知识的生成、组织与更新日益嵌入技术系统；其二，教育关系由以师
生互动为中心的人际关系结构，演变为技术深度介入的混合关系形态，技术逐步成为学习与交往的重要组织力量；
其三，教育整体结构由学校中心走向去边界化学习生态，学校的空间形态、功能边界与组织角色被系统性重塑。在此
背景下，数智时代宏观教育学的建构呈现出清晰的三层路径：在教育本体层，将教育界定为“技术—人类”共构的育
人活动；在教育组织层，重构学校作为技术共同体的理论形态；在教育制度层，发展以技术治理为核心的宏观教育治理。该理论框架旨在为数智时代中国宏观教育学的建构提供具有整体视野与理论自觉的系统化方案。
Abstract：
The advent of the digital-intelligence era is profoundly reshaping the modes of existence and the overall structure of education. Educational frameworks grounded in technological instrumentalism and anthropocentrism are increasingly inadequate for explaining the systemic changes that occur as technology shifts from an external condition to an ontological component of education itself. Therefore, this paper argues that the central task of macro-pedagogy in the digital-intelligence era does not lie in addressing the application of specific instructional technologies, but in grasping, at a holistic level, the deep structural reconfiguration currently unfolding in education. This structural transformation is manifested in three key dimensions. First, the epistemic foundation of education shifts from a text-centered mode of cultural inscription to an algorithmic form of knowledge based on data and computation, in which the generation, organization, and renewal of knowledge are increasingly embedded in technological systems. Second, educational relations evolves from interpersonal structures centered on teacher-student interaction to human-machine relationship deeply mediated by technology, with technology gradually becoming a key organizing force in learning and communication. Third, the overall structure of education transitions from a school-centered system to a de-bounded learning ecology, in which the spatial forms, functional boundaries, and organizational roles of schools are systematically reconfigured. Against this background, the construction of macro-pedagogy in the digital-intelligence era follows a three-level pathway: at the ontological level, education is redefined as an educative practice co-constituted by humans and technology; at the organizational level, schools are reconceptualized as technological communities; and at the institutional level, a macro-educational governance model driven by technology should be developed. This framework provides a systematic approach with a holistic perspective and theoretical reflexivity for the construction of macro-pedagogy in the digital-intelligence era.

科技教育大模型构建机理与实践进路——兼论教育科技人才一体化视域下的育人范式转型
[11-21]
刘智，李栋，龙陶陶，刘三女牙
DOI:
[10.15881/j.cnki.cn33-1304/g4.2026.02.002](http://dx.doi.org/10.15881/j.cnki.cn33-1304/g4.2026.02.002)
摘要
摘要：
教育科技人才一体化战略对科技创新人才培养提出了从知识传递向范式创新转型的系统性要求，生成 式人工智能为这一转型提供了关键驱动力，但其通用概率生成机制与科技教育所强调的实证严谨之间，存在内在的 认识论张力。本研究立足于“三位一体”的战略高度，系统解析科技教育大模型的构建机理与实践进路，依据“科技 （知识）—教育（方法）—人才（思维）”的同构逻辑，构建了其核心内涵与特征图谱。在此基础上，系统阐释了通过多模 态跨学科图谱映射、启发式教学策略植入与认知规律对齐构建专用模型的内在机理。进而，从场域赋能、行动协同与 循环反馈三个维度阐明了实践进路，以虚实互证环境、人机共生教学与素养循证评价推动育人范式转型。研究成果 为构建科教融通与人才强基的数智化教育新生态提供理论框架与实践指南。
Abstract：
The integrated strategy of education, science and technology, and talent development has imposed systemic requirements for transforming the cultivation of scientific and technological talent, from knowledge transmission to paradigm innovation. Generative artificial intelligence provides a key driving force for this transformation. However, an inherent epistemological tension exists between its general probabilistic generation mechanisms and the empirical rigor emphasized in science and technology education. From the strategic perspective of the “trinity” and following the isomorphic logic of “technology (knowledge)—education (method)—talent (thinking),” this study systematically examines large language models for science and technology education , delineating its core connotations and establishing a characteristic framework.On this basis, it elucidates the internal mechanisms for developing specialized models through multimodal interdisciplinary knowledge-graph mapping, the integration of heuristic instructional strategies, and alignment with cognitive laws. Furthermore, it proposes practical pathways across three dimensions—field empowerment, action coordination, and iterative feedback—to promote paradigm shift in talent cultivation through virtual-real integrated environments, human-machine collaborative teaching, and evidence-based competency evaluation.This study provides a theoretical framework and practical guidance for building a digital-intelligence educational ecosystem that integrates science and education while strengthening the foundation for talent development.

生成式人工智能在教育评价中的定位与实践——基于“对话”的隐喻视角
[22-29]
郑勤华，宋义深
DOI:
[10.15881/j.cnki.cn33-1304/g4.2026.02.003](http://dx.doi.org/10.15881/j.cnki.cn33-1304/g4.2026.02.003)
摘要
摘要：
随着智能时代的教育需求转向大规模因材施教，标准化的测
验式评价开始向多元化的生成式评价回归，而以对话为基础的生成式人工智能技术正切中了其中要义，具有在教育
评价全过程中提供支持的应用潜能。以相关领域的最新实践为基础，本研究基于“对话”的隐喻，将生成式人工智能在教育评价中的定位
总结为与评价材料对话的辅助技术、与评价过程对话的增强技术、与评价主体对话的载体技术三个阶段，形成了多
样化的对话模式与实践形态。基于总括性的理论视角，本研究构建的定位与实践框架，旨在为生成式人工智能在教
育评价领域的实践应用和研究探索提供参考。
Abstract：
As educational demands in the digital-intelligence era shift toward large-scale personalized learning, standardized test-based evaluation is gradually giving way to diversified, generative evaluation. Generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) grounded in dialogue aligns closely with this transformation and demonstrates strong potential to support the entire process of educational evaluation. Based on recent practices in related fields, this study conceptualizes the positioning of GenaAI in educational evaluation into three progressive stages from a metaphorical perspective of “dialogue”: as an assisting technology for “dialogue” with evaluation materials, as an augmentative technology for “dialogue” within the evaluation process, and as a mediating technology for “dialogue” with evaluation subjects. On this basis, this study constructs an overarching framework of GenAI’s positioning and practice, and explains the modes, forms, and effects of“dialogue” across different stages. The study not only offers theoretical insights into the the underlying mechanisms through which GenAI empowers educational evaluation, but also provides practical guidance for integrating GenAI into educational evaluation contexts in a stratified and classified manner.

面向复杂任务解决的多智能体协同学习环境构建——一项基于设计的研究
[30-42]
许家奇，唐陆禛，李子健，陈娜平，范逸洲
DOI:
[[DOI]10.15881/j.cnki.cn33-1304/g4.2026.02.004](http://dx.doi.org/[DOI]10.15881/j.cnki.cn33-1304/g4.2026.02.004)
摘要
摘要：
在教育智能化与规模化发展持续推进的背景下，教学环境需要支撑日益复杂的学习情境与能力培养需求。
然而，复杂任务解决在大规模教学实施过程中，往往面临情境难以真实还原、个性化脚手架供给不足等挑战。为此，
研究采用基于设计的研究方法，通过三轮迭代，构建了一个面向医学生临床诊断思维培养的多智能体协同学习环境。该系统由“AI 病
人”“AI 助手”“AI 带教医生”等角色构成，通过分工协作重建接近真实的问诊情境，并为学习者提供过程性思维支
架。研究分三阶段开展，共招募70 余位医学生参与多轮诊断任务，综合系统日志、问卷调查与过程挖掘分析发现：多
智能体协同的学习环境在高难度临床诊断任务中显著提升了诊断准确率，并促使成功诊断学习者在“证据权衡—认
知缺口识别—寻求脚手架”之间形成辩证认知循环。研究为多智能体协同促进复杂任务解决的学习环境设计，提供
了理论和实践支撑。

[93-102]
张奂奂
DOI:
[10.15881/j.cnki.cn33-1304/g4.2026.02.010](http://dx.doi.org/10.15881/j.cnki.cn33-1304/g4.2026.02.010)
摘要
摘要：
数字教材是推进高等教育数字化的重要载体。建构科学合理的高校教师数字教材使用评价指标体系，对于揭示教师使用行为特征、识别关键影响因素、促进数字教材推广具有重要意义。本研究采用混合研究设计：首先，基于文献型扎根理论方法进行三级编码，建构初始指标框架；其次，基于专家征询，运用模糊德尔菲法对指标进行筛选与修正，并结合层次分析法确定指标权重，形成包含4个一级指标、10个二级指标和35个三级指标的指标体系；再次，基于高校教师问卷数据，采用主成分分析与熵权法计算各指标权重，并检验其与专家赋权结果的一致性，同时分析不同教师群体的权重偏好差异，以识别权重优化方向。研究结果表明：该评价体系呈现出质量导向、需求导向与应用导向的三重特征；操作友好性、科学性、智能性与培训质量等指标为教师在真实使用情境中的关注重点；学科门类与高校类型是影响指标偏好的主要差异来源。基于此，研究从内容建设、技术赋能、用户体验、制度保障及评价更新五个方面提出建议，以期为高校数字教材的开发与推广提供参考依据。
Abstract：
Digital textbook serves as a key vehicle for advancing the digital transformation of higher education. Constructing a scientifically grounded evaluation index system for university teachers’ use of digital textbooks is essential for gaining deep insights into teachers’ usage behaviors, identifying key factors influencing their use, and promoting the adoption of digital textbooks. This study employs the mixed methods research design. First, a three-level coding process based on literature-grounded theory was conducted to construct an initial indicator framework. Subsequently, the fuzzy Delphi method was used to screen and refine the indicators, and the analytic hierarchy process was applied to determine indicator weights, resulting in a hierarchical index system comprising 4 first-level indicators, 10 second-level indicators, and 35 third-level indicators. Furthermore, based on survey data collected from university teachers, principal component analysis and the entropy weight method were employed to calculate indicator weights and examine their consistency with expert-derived weights. In addition, differences in weight preferences among different teacher groups were analyzed to identify directions for weight optimization. The results indicate that the evaluation system exhibits characteristics of quality orientation, demand orientation, and application orientation. Indicators such as user-friendliness, scientific rigor intelligence, and training quality are of primary concern of teachers in real practice. Furthermore, disciplinary categories and institutional types are identified as the main source of variation in indicator preference. Based on these findings, this study proposes recommendations from the perspectives of content quality development, technology empowerment, user experience, institutional support, and evaluation system iteration, with the aim of providing references for the development and promotion of digital textbooks in higher education.
