Overview
--------

*Intensive Care Medicine* serves as the premier publication platform facilitating the communication and exchange of cutting-edge research and ideas within the field of intensive care medicine on a comprehensive scale. This platform is designed for professionals engaged in intensive medical care, including intensivists, medical specialists, nurses, and other healthcare professionals dealing with adult ICU patients.

* ICM is the official journal of The European Society of Intensive Care Medicine.
* ICM aims to advance the understanding and practice of intensive care medicine among professionals in Europe and beyond.
* The journal provides a robust platform for disseminating current research findings and innovative ideas in intensive care medicine.
* Intensive Care Medicine publishes a wide array of content, including review articles, original research papers, letters, reviews, debates, and more. This diversity ensures comprehensive coverage of topics relevant to the intensive care community.
