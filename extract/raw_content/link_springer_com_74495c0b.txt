  ![]()We are proud to acknowledge that over 50% of the articles published in this journal in 2025 were related to one or more of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
* ### [Open Call for ETR&D Special Issue Proposals](/journal/11423/updates/27785108)
  
  ***Educational Technology Research and Development* (ETR&D)** invites **proposals for Special Issues** that address timely, significant, and innovative topics in the field of educational technology research and development. We welcome ongoing submissions of Special Issue proposals from scholars who are prepared to serve as Guest Editors and who can bring together diverse, high-quality contributions aligned with ETR&D’s mission. More...
* ### [Special Issue Call for Papers - Cognitive Dimensions in Digital Learning: Implications for Instructional Design](/journal/11423/updates/27741328)
  
  This special issue aims to assemble diverse perspectives on the cognitive dimensions of digital learning. We invite contributions that examine both theoretical and practical implications of cognitive factors in the design, implementation, and evaluation of digital learning environments. More...
* ### [Special Issue Call for Articles: Leveraging board games for learning](/journal/11423/updates/27741246)
  
  This special issue provides an opportunity for board game researchers to contribute to research and our understanding of learning. More...
* ### [Call for Proposals: Special Issue on “Replication Studies”](/journal/11423/updates/27721784)
  
  We invite proposals for replication studies in the fields of instructional design, learning sciences, and
