Abstract
--------

As artificial intelligence(AI) is utilised throughout society, the need to improve AI literacy as an essential competency, not only for specific experts but also for general citizens, is increasing. Therefore, several studies are being conducted on AI education, and attempts are being made to introduce it into the regular education curriculum. Although AI literacy has been emphasised as a direction for AI education, few assessment tools have been developed to measure the level of literacy to be achieved in stages. In this study, we developed and validated an AI literacy measurement tool for elementary school students. Based on AI education literature, we constructed an AI literacy content system for each school level and conducted two expert Delphi validations (*n* = 15). Based on this, we developed a set of 24 modular questions, conducted two rounds of expert content validation (*n* = 15), and preliminarily applied them to 287 sixth-grade students. The results were analysed for reliability based on the KR-20 (Kuder-Richardson Formula 20), difficulty, discrimination based on the item response theory, and correct percentages. Considering the discrimination and correctness results, we modified some items and applied them to an additional 293 sixth graders. The results showed improvements in the discrimination and precision rates. Thus, we concluded that the developed tool is valid and reliable for measuring AI literacy among elementary school students.

Appendix: AI Literacy Framework
-------------------------------

### Appendix: AI Literacy Framework

| Major Area | AI Literacy | Content Element | Content Element Guidance | Elementary School | Middle School | High School |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Understanding AI Concepts | What is AI? | Recognizing AI | Distinguish between technologies that use AI and those that do not. | - Exploring AI in everyday life  - Experience various programs that use AI (voice recognition, image recognition, generative AI-based services, etc.) | - Exploring AI concepts and features  - Distinguishing AI software | - Investigating the latest cases and exploring the scope and significance of AI usage |
|  | What can AI do? | Exploring intelligence | Discuss the difference between human and machine intelligence, and analyse and discuss what makes an entity intelligent. | - Exploring human intelligent behaviour | - Exploring intelligence in humans and machines | - Exploring various definitions of intelligence |
| Understanding How AI Works | How does AI work? | Exploring different ways to create AI | Understand that there are various ways to create AI and explore different technologies using AI. | - Compare how humans and computers learn  - Exploring the process of creating AI (understand the basic principles of machine learning by experiencing simple AI tools with machine learning) | - Understanding supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning  - Determine the right AI system to solve a problem | - Exploring AI in terms of search, reasoning, learning, perception, and generation  - Explore the concept of AI through examples of intelligent agents (AI systems) |
|  |  | Distinguish between weak and strong AI | Understand the concepts of weak and strong AI and explore societal changes upon the adoption of strong AI. | - Differentiate between weak and strong AI | - Exploring the possibilities of weak and strong AI | - Understanding the correct perception of the relationship between humans and AI |
|  |  | Distinguish between weak and strong AI | Distinguish between problems that AI excels at and those that are challenging for AI, and explore appropriate uses for AI. | - Exploring what AI does well | - Exploring what humans and AI do well  - Exploring AI’s dependence on data | - Investigating AI’s data dependence and technical limitations (e.g., understanding language and context, explainability)  - Exploring the potential applications of new AI technologies as they emerge |
|  |  | Exploring AI strengths and weaknesses | Explore the changes in society due to the advancements in AI technology. | - Exploring how everyday life is expected to change with AI technology | - Exploring changes in jobs and society due to AI technology | - Explore mid- to long-term changes in society due to AI technology |
|  |  | Exploring Knowledge Representation | Understand the concepts and methods of knowledge representation and explain examples of knowledge representation. | - Representing properties of things (objects)  - Representing knowledge in a mind map or table | - Expressing specific games (e.g., Omok) in natural language  - Recognize examples of tree structures and understand the relationships between components  - Using knowledge structures for predictions and judgments | - Graphically represent structures such as games and mind maps  -Recognize the elements of a graph and the relationships between them  - View knowledge structure representations and make predictions or judgments from them |
|  |  | Understanding decision making | Understanding AI’s reasoning and decision-making processes |  | - Exploring the classification process through decision trees  - Understand and illustrate the process of AI reasoning and decision-making. | - Recognizing the necessity of algorithms to understand model decision  - Understand and explore the AI decision-making process around you by seeing a decision example and understanding it (the entire process flow) |
|  |  | Understanding and utilizing data | Understanding data generation, collection, validation, and storage processes | - Exploring types and representations of data.  - Explore common types or shapes among data | - Understanding digital data representation  - Collecting and managing data  - Structure and interpret data | - Exploring common types and forms of data  - Explore methods for analysing data |
|  |  | Interpreting data critically | Understand that data cannot be used as is and requires interpretation, and explain how data affects results. | - Exploring the importance of data as applied to machine learning. | - Exploring the impact of data on machine learning performance  - Have an attitude that minimizes the potential for data-induced problems in AI learning.  - Explore the classification results and biases of machine learning models based on training data  - Explore the potential for bias and distortion in data | - Collect data so that it is unbiased  - Explore the uncertainty and potential for error in data  - Embrace multiple interpretations of data |
|  |  | Exploring machine learning | Understand the concept of machine learning and explore the process by which AI learns from data. | - Exploring simple concepts of machine learning | - Compare the differences between learning models and rule-based methods  - Train and evaluate image, text, and sound classification models using educational tools | - Understand how different supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning algorithms work  - Explore artificial neural networks and deep learning  - Understand foundation model  - Know what parameters are in a model  - Explore the importance of hardware in training models, including the role GPUs play. |
|  |  | Utilizing machine learning to solve problems | Understand the steps of machine learning and be able to utilize machine learning methods to solve problems. . | - Apply machine learning to solve real-world problems using artificial intelligence | - Train and evaluate image, text, and sound classification models using educational tools  - Implement and utilize machine learning models to solve problems | - Apply machine learning algorithms to problems, implementing and evaluating models.  - Implement and utilize machine learning models to solve problems |
|  |  | Understand the role of humans in AI development | Understand the role of humans in AI development  Recognize the need for a human role in programming AI systems, selecting and manipulating models. | - Understand the role of humans as users of AI | - Identify the roles of different people in creating an AI system | - Recognize the different perspectives and influences of different stakeholders on AI system  - Explore examples of machine learning models trained with human feedback (ChatGPT, Bard, etc.) |
|  |  | Exploring physical computing and artificial intelligence | Understand that AI can physically act on the world and implement the behavior of AI systems. | - Exploring examples of physical computing in real-life  - Hands-on experience with simple physical computing tools | - Exploring physical computing examples in daily life  - Explore real-world examples of physical computing with AI | - Analysing the composition and operating principles of physical computing systems  - Create artwork using physical computing with AI |
|  |  | Understand and utilize sensors | Understand what sensors are and recognize that AI uses sensors to perceive the world and that different sensors provide data for different types of representations and inferences about the world | - Compare the roles of human sensory organs and computer sensors  - Compare how human senses and computer sensors gather information  - Exploring the sensors, you see around you | - Explore examples of how information from different sensors can be used to make intelligent decisions. | - Explore the limitations and benefits of different computer sensors  - Explore radar, lidar, GPS, and accelerometer data  - Explore/imagine new sensors |
|  | How do People Understand AI? | Understand AI as programmed software | - Understand that AI can be created in the form of software.  - Explore how AI software is created through educational tool |
| Understanding AI Ethics | How should AI be used? | Exploring AI ethics | Explore and explain different perspectives on key ethical issues surrounding AI. | - Understand the impact of AI on society and explore ways to address social issues  - Manage and protect personal information safely and securely | - Explain the use and invasion of privacy of AI technologies  - Explore classification results and biases based on training data  - Understand and address bias, fairness, trustworthiness, and ethical dilemmas in AI | - Understand and address trustworthiness, fairness, reliability, and transparency in AI  - -Explore the technical challenges, fallibility, and solutions of AI  - Exploring ethical issues in generative AI  - Have an attitude towards collecting data legitimately and managing and protecting it securely |
