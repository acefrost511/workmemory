Y Combinator Winter 2026 Startups: YC W26 Batch Overview
========================================================

By[Pradeep Singh](https://startground.com/) 
Last Updated:April 2, 2026 





![]() 

Y Combinator has spent over two decades shaping the startup landscape. With more than $800 billion in combined market value created across its portfolio, a 6.5% unicorn rate, and a quarter of those unicorns exceeding $10 billion in valuation, it remains the gold standard for early-stage accelerators. Founders who go through YC are 45% more likely to raise a Series A compared to those who don’t.

The W26 batch continues that momentum. This cohort is one of the most AI-dense in YC history. From agentic workflows and LLM infrastructure to robotics, drug discovery, and defense — the startups below represent where the smartest founders believe the next decade of technology is heading.

**Key Takeaways:**

* **64% of the batch is B2B** – spanning AI infrastructure, developer tools, legal tech, and healthcare
* **Consumer startups make up just 5%** – signaling founders are betting heavily on enterprise AI
* **Healthcare is surging at nearly 10%** – with wearable tech and drug discovery leading the way
* **Legal tech is heating up at around 4%** – fueled by the rapid unicorn success of previous YC startups
* **AI is the foundation, not a feature** — the overwhelming majority of startups are AI-native from day one


Below is a complete breakdown of every startup in the batch, organized by category. Whether you’re an investor scouting deal flow, a founder looking for collaborators, or simply curious about what’s next in tech, this is your definitive reference.
