YC AI Startups 2026: The Complete Batch Breakdown
=================================================

Y Combinator's 2026 batches are roughly 60% AI companies — up from 40% in 2024. The accelerator has backed some of the biggest names in AI (Cursor, Perplexity, Scale AI, Runway), and the latest batches show where the smart money thinks AI is heading next: vertical agents, infrastructure plays, and AI-native workflows replacing entire SaaS categories.

This guide tracks every notable AI company from recent YC batches. We've added context you won't find on YC's own directory: which founders have appeared on AI podcasts, what traction metrics are public, and which companies are gaining momentum based on community signals.

How to Use This Guide
---------------------

For each company, we include:

* **What they do** — Product description and target market
* **Batch** — When they participated in YC
* **Funding** — Known rounds and investors
* **Why they matter** — Competitive positioning and what makes them interesting

How to Get Into YC
------------------

### For AI Startups

1. **Apply early** - YC rolls, apply anytime
2. **Build in public** - Share demos, metrics
3. **Focus on growth** - Show clear trajectory
4. **Demo day matters** - Prepare extensively

### What YC Looks For

* **Founder quality** - Can you execute?
* **Large market** - Can this be big?
* **Technical differentiation** - What's your moat?
* **Traction** - Users, revenue, growth
