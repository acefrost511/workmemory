49 predictions about edtech, innovation, and–yes–AI in 2026
===========================================================

January 5, 2026![]()

What’s in store for K-12 schools for 2026? In a recent piece by eSchool News, [Alliance Executive Director David Samuelson](https://alliancefordecisioneducation.org/learn/about-the-alliance/team/david-samuelson/), alongside other K-12 industry experts, stakeholders, and educators, shared insights about what 2026 will bring for education. From AI and edtech to tighter budgets and changing enrollment, see what topics are demanding attention in the year ahead.

[Read the full piece on eSchool News.](https://www.eschoolnews.com/innovative-teaching/2026/01/01/draft-2026-predictions/)

Continue learning about how Decision Education intersects with current topics, like AI and workforce readiness, [here](https://alliancefordecisioneducation.org/resources/decision-education-current-issues/).
